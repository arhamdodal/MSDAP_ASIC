Course algo:

y(n) = 2^-1(
    u4 + 2^-1(
        u3 + 2^-1(
                 u1)))

compute all u from coefficients and Rj
    coefficients- gives relative position of data and sign
    Rj- number of data points associated with each u bucket

start with 0
for every u
    add u to accumulator
    shift left by one bit
